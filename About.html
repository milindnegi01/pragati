About Us

Your trusted partner in comprehensive fire safety solutions since 2015.

Our Story

Founded with a mission to protect lives and property, Pragati Fire Protection Services has grown to become a leading provider of comprehensive fire safety solutions.

Who We Are

Pragati Fire Protection Services was established in 2015 by a team of experienced fire safety professionals with a vision to provide comprehensive fire protection solutions that prioritize safety, reliability, and customer satisfaction.

Over the years, we have grown from a small local business to a trusted regional provider of fire safety services, serving clients across multiple industries including commercial, residential, healthcare, hospitality, and government sectors.

Our team of certified professionals brings decades of combined experience in fire protection, ensuring that every project we undertake meets the highest standards of quality and compliance with all relevant regulations and codes.

Our Mission & Values

We are guided by a clear mission and strong core values that shape everything we do.

Our Mission

To protect lives and property by providing the highest quality fire protection services and solutions that exceed industry standards and customer expectations.

We are committed to promoting a culture of safety through education, training, and the implementation of effective fire protection systems that help prevent, detect, and suppress fires.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and respected fire protection service provider, recognized for our expertise, integrity, and commitment to excellence in every aspect of our business.

We envision a future where fire-related incidents are minimized through proactive safety measures, advanced technology, and comprehensive education, contributing to safer communities and workplaces.

Our Evolution

The FireGuard Story

From humble beginnings to becoming a leading fire protection service provider, our journey has been defined by growth, innovation, and a commitment to excellence.

01
2015

Foundation

Pragati Fire Protection Services was founded by Mr Gajendra Singh Raghav with a small team of 3 employees, focusing initially on fire extinguisher sales and maintenance for local businesses.

02
2017

Expansion of Services

We expanded our service offerings to include fire alarm system installation and maintenance, growing our team to 15 certified technicians and engineers.

03

Regional Growth

We opened our second office, expanding our service area to cover the entire state and adding sprinkler system design and installation to our service portfolio.

04

Industry Recognition
